Ask Italian

As a large and clearly defined company, Ask Italian already has clear brand guidelines. My task was to explore new directions to take within these guidelines. I worked primarily with two branches: Haslemere and Taunton. By improving the brand cohesion of their social media posts, it helped them reach the local community in a more official capacity than they were able to do before. Ask Italian has a predefined set of brand colours; however, there was still room to expand their palette through the use of associated tones and hues. Their advertising often features illustrations of fruit and plants therefore, I created additional illustrations, within the original art style.

Line Art

A lot of the branding centres around this style of line art, so it can be used in combination with the colours and font to create marketing material that is clearly identifiable with the brand.

Covid-19

During the pandemic, the company was quick to take action with the implementation of advice signs. I created this and a handful of others to be shared on social media.
