Ask Italian

As a large and clearly defined company, Ask Italian already has clearly defined brand guidliines. Creating content for them as such was about exploring new directions to take these guidliines. I worked primarily with two branches, Haslemere and Taunton. By improving the brand coheshion of their social media posts it helped them reach the local comunity in a more official capacity than they were able to do before. Ask italian has a predefined set of brand colours, but there was still room to expoand through the use of associated tones and hues. Their advertising often fetures illustrations of fruit and plants and so I created aditional illistrations, within the originals art style.

Line Art

Alot of the branding centers around this style of line, so it can be used in combination with the colours and font to create marketing meterial that is clearly identifiable with the brand
