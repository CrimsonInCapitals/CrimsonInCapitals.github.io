Aspinal of London

In accordance with discussions between the client and myself, I developed alternative brand symbols for use on products and new business ventures. The client wanted to explore the way the lion door knocker, featured on some of their products, could be integrated into the shield found on the original logo. The purpose of these designs was to be used for a sister company, called 'House of Aspinal' . I designed 2 sets of logos and an umbrella mark, featuring the iconic feather and two variation 'button' designs. For the creation of the new wordmarks, I designed a font based on the unique 'Aspinal' and 'London' typography that could be used for future projects.
